About Us

OGallery is a free and open-source image gallery application owned by its contributors. It is licensed under the GNU General Public License (GPL) version 3.0 or later.

OGallery is developed and maintained by a community of contributors passionate about creating a powerful and user-friendly image gallery solution. Our goal is to provide a reliable tool for organizing, viewing, and sharing images, while promoting the principles of open-source software. We welcome contributions from developers, designers, and users who share our vision. Whether you're interested in contributing code, reporting bugs, or suggesting new features, we invite you to join our community and help us improve OGallery for everyone.
